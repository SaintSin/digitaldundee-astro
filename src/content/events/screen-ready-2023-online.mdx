Are you a location or business that wants to work with film and TV production? Perhaps you already do. Join us online for our <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;outline:none;">free</strong> morning event on Wednesday, 6th December at 10am. We’re excited to welcome:

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;outline:none;">
    Tim Maskell, Scottish Location Services
  </strong>

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;outline:none;">
    Mat Bett, Abertay University, Virtual Production
  </strong>

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;outline:none;">
    Joshua Ryan-Saha, Director, Traveltech, Edinburgh Futures Institute
  </strong>

We’ll be hearing about media production which brings spending on locations and services and promotes the region, attracts tourism and boosts the regional economy. It’s also a chance to find out about the growing new trend of virtual production and what this means for locations. We will also hear about the <a style="background-color:transparent;box-sizing:border-box;color:rgb(221, 84, 61);outline:none;text-decoration:none;transition:all 0.3s ease 0s;" href="https://www.traveltech.scot/project/traveltech-for-tay-cities" rel="noreferrer">Tay Cities Region Traveltech Challenge</a> for tourism businesses to try out all kinds of technology to help with areas like getting staff and promotion.
