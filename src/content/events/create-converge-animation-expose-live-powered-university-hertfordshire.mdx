**Virtual and Free Join us live on Youtube as Digital Dundee's Create Converge is taking part in Animation Expose Live with The Upshot, a review of what we're all about and how we can help you with collaboration across creative, digital and tech.**

It all kicks off at 11am UK / 12pm CE

Create Converge is up at 1pm UK / 2pm CE.

The brilliant line up of speakers at the event includes:

John Stevenson, co-director of Kung-Fu Panda and director of Sherlocke Gnomes

Floyd Billingy, CG supervisor and Lead Environment Artist at Axis Studios

Aya Suzuki, story/layout/animator/lecturer with credits such as The Illusionist, Wolf Children and 101 Dalmations.

List for free at the [Create Converge community](https://www.createconverge.eu/business).

Follow us at [Linkedin Create Converge](https://www.linkedin.com/company/createconverge).

Join our [Linkedin Connect Create Converge Group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/12124570/).

## How to watch and take part

Keep an eye on [http://uhanimation.co.uk](http://uhanimation.co.uk/) for the soon-to-be-announced schedule for the day and the full details of how to watch the proceedings live online. Then on 22nd June you can point your browser in the direction of our live stream, join in for chats with the industry guests and graduating students and virtually meet your next generation of junior artists.

Download the Create Converge book [Story Telling Beyond the Screen.](https://filmbyaarhus.dk/new-publication-storytelling-beyond-the-screen/)
