#### 18th November 4pm @ Malmaison, dundee

Calling all games students and developers - are you currently a games student, or are you in the process of developing the next Minecraft, Grand Theft Auto or Bloons?

We are hosting an afternoon with Henderson Loggie accountants, David Hamilton of Ninja Kiwi and Jordan Hastings from Astrodreamer Studio to provide you with first-hand experience from those within the games industry and that all-important legal and accountancy advice required to make your Games Start-up a success!

The seminar will cover the following:-

The Law: 5 things every games start-up must do – Richard Wilson, Blackadders

The Numbers: Tax and accounting considerations – Grant Snedden, Henderson Loggie

Lessons learned in the industry - David Hamilton, Ninja Kiwi

A Start-up’s Journey – Jordan Hastings, Astrodreamer Studio

Date: Thursday 18th November 2021

Time: 4pm

Location: Malmaison Hotel, Dundee

Cost: FREE

NB: Places are limited to 30 on a first come first served basis.
