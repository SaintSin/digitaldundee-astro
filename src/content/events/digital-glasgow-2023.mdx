Glasgow is one of the fastest-growing tech centres in Europe. According to a recent report from Dealroom, the city is bucking a largely downward European trend for startup and scaleup venture capital investment. It has entered what Scotland’s chief entrepreneur, Mark Logan, recently called a “virtuous cycle”, with a growing cluster of tech companies starting to reinforce each other’s success.
