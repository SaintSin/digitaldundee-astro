**A chance to socialise and connect with other startup founders in the Dundee & Tayside region. **Open to everyone involved with tech startups in the region. Come along and try a selection of amazing cheeses from The Cheesery and meet other founders and members of the CodeBase team.

This will be an opportunity to meet like-minded folk working within the Dundee tech sector, share ideas, learning and get inspired. You will also have the opportunity to share what you need from the community and how you could potentially help others.
