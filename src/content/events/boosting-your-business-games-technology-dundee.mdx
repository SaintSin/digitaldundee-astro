## Boosting Your Business with Games Technology-Dundee

#### Free - In Person - Book your place below

Business Gateway is hosting a workshop at Abertay University’s Cyber Quarter. Discover how games technology can transform your business, from training to marketing, and learn how local gaming talent can enhance key areas of your operations.

What to Expect:

Dundee is famous for its video game industry, but did you know that gaming technologies have diverse applications across the business world? From the Tesco Clubcard to RSS Discovery’s virtual tour, gaming technology is everywhere!

Join us to explore how game-based technology can enhance various aspects of your business, including:

• Employee training

• Data visualization

• Employee health and wellbeing

• Product prototyping

• Marketing

With an expert panel and numerous local gaming companies in attendance, this event is a fantastic opportunity to collaborate with local talent and support your business’s continued success.
