The University is hosting the first [Tayside Regional Improvement Collaborative (TRIC) Digifest](https://blogs.glowscotland.org.uk/as/tricdigitalevent/), bringing together educational practitioners and young people from across Tayside and partner agencies from across Scotland and beyond.

The TRIC Digifest will provide opportunities to access high-quality and highly engaging professional learning as well as opportunities to be inspired by colleagues and young people from across the Regional Improvement Collaborative, showcasing exciting approaches to using digital technology to enhance learning and teaching.

TRIC Digifest is free for everyone to attend and contributes towards professional learning.

There will be representation from Nursery, Primary and Secondary schools from across the Collaborative and guest speakers from The University of Dundee, Code Club Scotland, Google Scotland and Education Scotland.

To book your place, browse the festival programme, note the workshops you want to attend [and then use the booking form](https://blogs.glowscotland.org.uk/as/tricdigitalevent/workshop-booking-form/) to register your interest.

Early booking is recommended to guarantee a place.

Refreshments will be provided throughout the day, including pastries and hot bacon rolls on arrival and a sandwich lunch. Please indicate your dietary requirements on the workshop booking form.
