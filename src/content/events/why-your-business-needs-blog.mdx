What to expect from this webinar

This webinar will provide you with the knowledge and understanding you need to create a blog that promotes your business.

Business Gateway will cover:

- What a 'blog' is, how to set one up and what it takes to make them work

- Other small and medium sized businesses who already blog successfully and what you can learn from them

- Options for monetising a blog

- How to promote your blog

- How your blog can help move your website higher up search engine ranking

- Understanding analytics and measuring the impact of your blog
