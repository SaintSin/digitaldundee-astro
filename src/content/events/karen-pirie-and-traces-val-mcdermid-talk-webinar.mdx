Go (well) behind the scenes with the publishing world’s ‘Queen of Crime’

Following filming of Karen Pirie, Traces and the upcoming Karen Pirie 2 in Dundee and Fife, writer, Val McDermid is hosting a prestigious talk at the University of Dundee on Wednesday, 11th December. It will also be online.

Tickets for the Leverhulme Research Centre for Forensic Science Annual Lecture are free but must be booked in advance at the Eventbrite link below.
