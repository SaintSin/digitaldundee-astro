Tayside TechFest is the region’s flagship celebration of innovation, collaboration, and digital excellence.

Tayside TechFest is the region’s flagship celebration of innovation, collaboration, and digital excellence. Bringing together startups, scale-ups, industry leaders, students, and tech enthusiasts, TechFest showcases the vibrant digital ecosystem across Dundee, Angus, Perth & Kinross.

Expect a packed programme of Inspiring talks from tech pioneers, networking opportunities with investors and innovators, Hands-on demos, Showcases of cutting-edge projects and local success stories

Whether you're building the next big thing, exploring career paths in tech, or just curious about the future of digital, Tayside TechFest is your gateway to connect, learn, and grow.

Join us as we spotlight the talent, energy, and ambition driving Tayside’s digital future.
