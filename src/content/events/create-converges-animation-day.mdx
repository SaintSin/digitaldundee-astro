One from our Create Converge partner in Germany.

Digital Dundee is a partner in Create Converge about using creative, digital, tech to show, tell and sell. Join the community, get help to find collaborators and get listed for free at [https://createconverge.eu/business](https://createconverge.eu/business)

On 4th June 2021, the 37th Kurzfilm Festival Hamburg presents Animation Day with the kind support of the Hamburg Chamber of Commerce, in cooperation with the North Sea Interreg project Create Converge and in collaboration with AG Animationsfilm. In a combination of talks and screenings, an entire day will be devoted to the current state of the animation field. The industry events are aimed in particular at film students and anyone else interested in animation.
