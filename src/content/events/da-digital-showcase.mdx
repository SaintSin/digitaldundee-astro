**19th-21st November @ DCA**

Come along and meet lecturers and students from Dundee and Angus College. Find out about studying Computing and Esports and take part in a variety of fun games and competitions: Design a game Character, Esports and Gaming Jargon, VR game competitions, Retro games Competitions, Mini Gaming Tournament and a Robot Assault course.
