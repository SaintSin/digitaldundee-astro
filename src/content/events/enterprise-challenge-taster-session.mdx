Are you interested in taking part in the Enterprise Challenge? Do you have a great business idea and want to turn it into reality? Whether you're looking to build a team, gain valuable insights, or take the first steps toward launching your venture, this Taster Session is the perfect opportunity to make it happen.

Join the **Centre for Entrepreneurship - University of Dundee **taster session to explore what the Enterprise Challenge is all about. Visit the Centre for Entrepreneurship, meet students and staff from diverse disciplines, and dive into key business fundamentals. This session is your gateway to developing skills that will benefit you in any career while sparking your entrepreneurial journey.

If you then choose to apply for the Enterprise Challenge, you'll gain access to hands-on workshops designed to push your boundaries, grow your business idea, and ignite your passion for innovation.

For those with a business idea, this is a fantastic opportunity to network, build a team, or pursue your idea individually. Experience the complete journey from ideation to realisation!

Spaces for the Enterprise Challenge are limited, with priority given to those who already have a business idea. [Applications for Semester 2 are now open](https://www.dundee.ac.uk/entrepreneurship/submit-your-application-enterprise-challenge), with the program kicking off on **Thursday 30 January**.
