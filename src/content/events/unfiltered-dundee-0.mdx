### What is it?

Open to everyone, head to the Abertay cyberQuarter to connect with members of the local tech and business community. Come along for coffee, breakfast and networking!

### What can I expect?

Techies, creatives, academics, freelancers, CodeBase staff and anyone interested in meeting peers within the Dundee tech ecosystem and beyond!
