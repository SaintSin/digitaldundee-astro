Open to everyone, join us at the Abertay cyberQuarter to connect with members of the local tech community. Come for a coffee, networking and build connections.

Unfiltered is an opportunity to meet and connect with people from across the tech ecosystem. We like to run Unfiltered with minimal structure, to allow conversations to flow naturally over a coffee. Unfiltered takes place on the first Wednesday of the month. Please feel free to drop in at any time between 08:30 to 10:30 and stay on to hotdesk.

We'll be on the ground floor of the Abertay cyberQuarter: just across from the main campus and right next to Annie's cafe.
