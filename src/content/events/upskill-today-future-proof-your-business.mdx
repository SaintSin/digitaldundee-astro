Calling all **electricians, plumbers, car mechanics/technicians and trades businesses** working with hybrid/electric vehicles (EV), EV charging installation points, hydrogen vehicles, robots, cobots or heat pump systems.

Explore our cutting-edge facilities, equipment and green skills training at **The Skills Academy** at Michelin Scotland Innovation Parc, designed to elevate your skills in current & future technologies.

We will also be launching our **Green Skills Fund** at this event which provides businesses based in Dundee with **fully-funded** Electric/Hybrid and Hydrogen vehicle training.

**Free training through the Green Skills Fund:**

- **EV Repair and Maintenance:** Essential skills for mechanics and technicians.

- **EV Charging Installation:** Training for electricians to install charging points.

- **Hydrogen Vehicle Awareness:** Training to understand and safely work around hydrogen vehicles.

- **Electric/Hybrid Vehicle Awareness:** For drivers, valeters, sales personnel, and support staff to safely operate around EVs.

- **Decarbonise Your Fleet: **A tailored course for business owners and managers, in partnership with The Service Design Academy, to help you develop a plan to decarbonise your fleet of vehicles such as delivery vans, company cars, work vans, taxis etc.

The Green Skills Fund is exclusively for Dundee-based businesses and their employees, helping to meet the growing demand for electric and hydrogen vehicles, support the decarbonisation of transport, and thrive in a greener economy.
