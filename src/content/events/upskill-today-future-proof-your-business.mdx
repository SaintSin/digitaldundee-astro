Calling all <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">electricians, plumbers, car mechanics/technicians and trades businesses</strong> working with hybrid/electric vehicles (EV), EV charging installation points, hydrogen vehicles, robots, cobots or heat pump systems.

Explore our cutting-edge facilities, equipment and green skills training at <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">The Skills Academy</strong> at Michelin Scotland Innovation Parc, designed to elevate your skills in current & future technologies.

We will also be launching our <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">Green Skills Fund</strong> at this event which provides businesses based in Dundee with <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">fully-funded</strong> Electric/Hybrid and Hydrogen vehicle training.

<strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">
  Free training through the Green Skills Fund:
</strong>

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">
    EV Repair and Maintenance:
  </strong>
  Essential skills for mechanics and technicians.

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">
    EV Charging Installation:
  </strong>
  Training for electricians to install charging points.

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">
    Hydrogen Vehicle Awareness:
  </strong>
  Training to understand and safely work around hydrogen vehicles.

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">
    Electric/Hybrid Vehicle Awareness:
  </strong>
  For drivers, valeters, sales personnel, and support staff to safely operate
  around EVs.

- <strong style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">
    Decarbonise Your Fleet:{" "}
  </strong>
  A tailored course for business owners and managers, in partnership with The
  Service Design Academy, to help you develop a plan to decarbonise your fleet
  of vehicles such as delivery vans, company cars, work vans, taxis etc.

The Green Skills Fund is exclusively for Dundee-based businesses and their employees, helping to meet the growing demand for electric and hydrogen vehicles, support the decarbonisation of transport, and thrive in a greener economy.
