A two-day industry conference in Dundee, the heart of Scotland’s gaming heritage, focusing on the cutting edge of game design, development, publishing, funding, and more.

Find out more about the speakers at [Scottish Games Week](https://gamesweek.scot/#speakers). Just a few of the amazing speakers include Brentton White of North Sea Games, Lisa Bennett of Dimoso, David Mullich of Cinemai and Simon Meek of Stacking Chairs.
