<strong style="-webkit-text-stroke-width:0px;background-color:rgb(255, 255, 255);box-sizing:border-box;color:rgb(57, 54, 79);font-family:"Neue Plak", -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Roboto, "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Tahoma, Arial, sans-serif;font-size:14px;font-style:normal;font-variant-caps:normal;font-variant-ligatures:normal;letter-spacing:normal;orphans:2;text-align:start;text-decoration-color:initial;text-decoration-style:initial;text-decoration-thickness:initial;text-indent:0px;text-transform:none;white-space:normal;widows:2;word-spacing:0px;">Gain insights into Scotland's telecoms ecosystem and network with potential collaborators ahead of the Mobile World Congress (MWC).</strong>

<strong style="box-sizing:border-box;font-weight:bolder;padding-top:0px;">
  The Scotland 5G Centre
</strong>
is delighted to host this networking event in Glasgow designed to support
Scottish companies and start-ups involved in advanced communications and future
telecoms.

<strong style="box-sizing:border-box;font-weight:bolder;padding-top:0px;">
  Join us on Tuesday, February 11, 2025, at 10:00 AM
</strong>
for the
<em style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">
  Connecting Scotland’s Telecoms Event
</em>
at the Technology and Innovation Centre. This full-day event will feature
networking opportunities, engaging discussions, and valuable insights into
Scotland’s telecom industry.

<strong style="box-sizing:border-box;font-weight:bolder;padding-top:0px;">
  In partnership with Innovate UK (IUK)
</strong>
, this event offers a unique platform for businesses delivering innovative
solutions in the future telecoms market. Participants can connect with Innovate
UK representatives and other key players in the sector.

Attendees will gain insights into Scotland's vibrant telecoms ecosystem and explore collaboration opportunities with potential partners ahead of the <em style="box-sizing:border-box;padding-top:0px;">Mobile World Congress (MWC) Barcelona</em> in March 2025.

The event will showcase a diverse range of speakers. Additionally, participants will have the chance to deliver short presentations highlighting their cutting-edge digital products and services, enhancing visibility and fostering partnerships.

A pivotal event that brings Scotland’s innovation closer to the world stage—don’t miss the opportunity to be part of it!
