For the fourth year, the StartUp Britain’s StartUp Bus Tour is set to tour Britain with a bus full of start-up experts and champions. Providing inspiration and support to tens of thousands of aspiring and early-stage entrepreneurs, with the overall aim to help boost the number of businesses started in 2015 to a record 600,000.
