Dundee and Tay Cities Region own eRena Sports is hosting the Phase 25 eSports Festival and Conference in Edinburgh in September 2025.

Phase 25 is brought to you by eRena Sports which merged with eSports Scotland in 2024. The company is headed by Ninja Kiwi's David Hamilton which merged with eSports Scotland led by James Hood.

Tay Cities Deal Tay5G was delighted to support eSports Scotland League 4 produced by James who welcomed the merger as a hugely positive development for the company and eSports in Scotland and beyond.
