Dundee's Cobra Mobile have seen their game Cobi Hoops featured on DigitalTrends "Best iMessage" games list for 2020.

Mark Ettle says: "We made Cobi Hoops in a few weeks by focusing in on making a quick bit-size gaming challenge you could play and send to friends...A few million downloads later and it's still going strong in 2020."

Cobi Hoops is a basketball iMessage game that presents you with the challenge of seeing how many baskets you can score in 30 seconds.

Read more about Cobi Hoops, Cobi Hoobs 2 and see the full list at [Digital Trends](https:// https://www.digitaltrends.com/mobile/best-imessage-games/?fbclid=IwAR3k6PhUFQtfru5u6kCMmMQjAwP_Ko-isR6oCFxMG8cpLVl1DJNM6AINJJo).
